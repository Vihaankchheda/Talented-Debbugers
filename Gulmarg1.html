Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a town, hill station, and popular tourist
destination located in the Baramulla district of the
Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir. It is
situated at an altitude of 2730 m above sea level
and is surrounded by snow-covered lofty Himalayas,
meadows of flowers, deep ravines, and evergreen
forested valleys1. Gulmarg is also a popular skiing
destination and has the world’s second-highest
Gondola ride1.

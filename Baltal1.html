Baltal

Baltal is a camping ground for pilgrims, located 75 km
from Ganderbal town in the Ganderbal district of
Jammu and Kashmir, India1. It is situated 93 km from
the capital city Srinagar and 15 km north of Sonamarg
on the Sind River at the base of Zojila pass1. The
valley is set at an altitude of 2,743 m.

Baltal serves as the base camp for pilgrims on their
onward journey to the Amarnath Caves, which are 14 km
away. The site is seen with pitched tents meant for the
pilgrims to spend the night1. Baltal is one hour away
from Sonmarg by taxi or bus1. It can also be reached
from Pahalgam, 24 km away, in about 1 day by foot.

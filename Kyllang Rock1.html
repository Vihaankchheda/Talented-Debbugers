Kyllang Rock

Kyllang Rock is a geological marvel located in West Khasi
Hills district of Meghalaya. It is a giant grey dome that
juts out of the pine forests and is about a thousand
feet wide at its most prominent girth 1. The rock is
also known as Lum Kyllang and is steeped in folklore.
According to Khasi legend, it is believed to be the
result of a battle between two old gods, Kyllang and
Symper, where they threw large rocks and mud at each
other 1.The rock formation is surrounded by rare white
rhododendrons scattered across the depressions and
bushes at the base of the dome. Old oaks and various
native flora add to the natural diversity of the area
.There is a path which you can climb along with rest
stops and summer houses along the way 1. The view from
the top of the rock is spectacular – from green woods
and quaint villages to mesmerizing sunrises and
sunsets if you time your hike accordingly.

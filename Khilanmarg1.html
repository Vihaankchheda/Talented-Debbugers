Khilanmarg

Khilanmarg is a small valley located in Jammu and
Kashmir, India1. It is situated about 6 kilometers
away from Gulmarg. The valley is set at a 6 km
walk and 2000 feet above Gulmarg. This place is
surrounded by panoramic glimpses of some of the
tallest Himalayan peaks.

The meadow is carpeted with flowers in the spring,
making it a delightful place to be in. It is the
site for Gulmarg’s winter ski runs and offers a
view of the surrounding peaks and over the Kashmir
Valley. It’s a 600 meters ascent from Gulmarg to
Khilanmarg.

Khilanmarg is a great place for offbeat travel
enthusiasts as the place is not directly reachable
on vehicles. One has to either walk from Gulmarg or
take a pony to reach this place3. It is covered with
blossoming and aromatic flowers in spring and is a
hot shot destination for skiing enthusiasts in
winter.

Tapola

Tapola is surrounded by hills and deep trees and is located on the banks of the Koyna River.
The community is renowned for its verdant greenery, clear waterways, and various water sports,
including swimming, boating, and kayaking. Tapola Lake, Shivsagar Lake, and Vasota Fort are
well-known tourist destinations nearby.

Venna Lake

Venna Lake is a popular tourist destination in Mahabaleshwar, Maharashtra, India. The water is
encircled by woods on both sides.The lake's shores are lined with a variety of tiny cafes.
Venna Lake also serves as one of Mahabaleshwar's most stunning and attractive lakes but
also serves several different purposes. It serves as the township's primary supply of
water. It regarded as one of Mahabaleshwar's most popular tourist destinations.

Lingamala Falls

The Lingamala Falls is an ideal spot for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts. It is a
great trekking, rock climbing, rappelling and camping spot. The area around the falls is
lush and green, with various flora and fauna. Visitors can also explore several nearby
caves in the area.

Sunset Point

Sunset Point is a popular tourist spot in Mahabaleshwar, Maharashtra, India. It is also
known as Mumbai Point or Bombay Point. The point is on the old road to Mumbai and is
the oldest point of Mahabaleshwar. It has a large open space with a bandstand and is
an ideal place for late afternoon picnic. The point is famous for its clear and nice
view of the setting sun. People gather around in the evening to watch the sun set.
It’s worth visiting if you’re in Mahabaleshwar.

Shillong

Shillong, the capital of the state is a busy, trendy and
energetic city with a vibrant population. There is a lot
of British influence in the architecture of Shillong.
With the beautiful hills around and the number of lakes,
Shillong is referred to as the ‘Scotland of East’
Shillong is the capital of Meghalaya, a state in
northeastern India. It is located on the Shillong
Plateau, at an elevation of 4,990 feet (1,520 meters).
Here are some interesting facts about Shillong:
1. Natural beauty: Shillong is known for its
breathtakingly beautiful golf courses, crystal clear
lakes, and numerous waterfalls 13. The city is
surrounded by hills and offers a serene environment to
relax and unwind.
2. Gateway to Meghalaya: Shillong is the gateway to
Meghalaya, a state famous for its heavy rainfall, caving,
tallest waterfalls, beautiful landscapes, and amazing
people and culture.
3. Music capital of India: Shillong is also known as the
music capital of India as many prominent musicians have
hailed from this place.
4. Scotland of the East: Shillong is often referred to as
the “Scotland of the East” due to its European settlers
of Scotland and its similarity with Scotland in terms of
natural beauty and climate.
5. Cleanest village in Asia: Mawlynnong, located near
Shillong, has been declared as the cleanest village in
Asia.

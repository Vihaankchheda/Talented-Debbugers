Pahalgam

Pahalgam is a popular tourist destination located in
the Anantnag district of the Indian-administered
union territory of Jammu and Kashmir12. Here are
some key details about Pahalgam:

Location: Pahalgam is situated on the banks of the
Lidder River, amidst the great Himalayan range. It’s
located about 90km away from Srinagar3 and 100
kilometers from Srinagar.

Elevation: The town is located at an altitude of
2130 m above sea level.

Population: As of 2011, the population of Pahalgam
was 5,9221.

Languages: The official languages are Kashmiri,
Urdu, Hindi, Dogri, and English.

Tourism: Pahalgam is known for its awe-inspiring
vistas, lush green meadows, dense forests, and
pristine waters. It’s also the starting point
for the annual Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage.

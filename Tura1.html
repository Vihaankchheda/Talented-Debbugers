Tura

Tura is a municipality in the West Garo Hills district of the Indian
state of Meghalaya1. It is one of the largest towns in Meghalaya
and is located in the foothills of the Nokrek range of Garo Hills.

Tura is also an eco-tourist hub of the state. It’s close to
popular tourist destinations such as Balpakram, Nokrek, and Siju
Cave Chitmang Peak. There are many waterfalls and streams in
Tura, including Rongbangdare, Pelgadare, Gandrak Falls,
Rangolwari, Nokmawari, Ganol, and Dachima.

The town is 220 kilometres from the nearest city Guwahati and
is also the district headquarter of the West Garo Hills district.
The capital city of Shillong is 323 kilometres away1. The
border of Bangladesh Dalu, is situated at a proximity of 50km.

Gopalgad Hill

Gopalgad Hill is a historical fort located in Anjanvel Village, Ratnagiri district,
Maharashtra, India. The fort is situated on a hill near the sea and is spread over
an area of 7 acres. It is a very beautiful and scenic fort that offers a panoramic
view of the surrounding area. The walls to the south have been broken down to create
an entrance from there. The trekking path starts from the hillock west of the
Anjanvel village. There is also a very safe and wide road up to the fort 1.





Bhatye Beach

Bhatye Beach is a popular tourist destination located in Ratnagiri district of
Maharashtra, India. The beach is approximately 2 kilometers long and offers
stunning views of the Arabian Sea 12. The beach is known for its calmness and
cleanliness. It is also a great spot for swimming and sunbathing.The beach
is surrounded by several restaurants and food stalls that offer delicious local
cuisine. If you are looking for a peaceful getaway from the hustle and bustle
of city life, Bhatye Beach is definitely worth a visit.

Ratnadurg Fort

Ratnadurg Fort, also known as Bhagawati Fort, is a historical fort located in Ratnagiri
district of Maharashtra, India. The fort was built during the Bahamani period and was
later won by Maratha King Shivaji from the hands of Adil Shah of Bijapur in 1670. The fort
is spread over an area of 120 acres and is surrounded by the Arabian Sea on three sides.
The fort is a popular tourist attraction due to the Bhagawati Temple inside the fort. The
temple was renovated in 1950. The fort offers a panoramic view of the sea and is a great
place to visit for history buffs and nature lovers alike.
